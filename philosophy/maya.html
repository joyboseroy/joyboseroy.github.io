Advaita Vedanta
In Advaita philosophy, maya is related to the concept of atman, with some texts suggesting that atman is the cause of maya (cite: edge_019163). Atman is also described as being qualified as an aspect of maya, with maya being seen as a force that creates distinctions and separations (cite: edge_020026). However, Advaita philosophy also emphasizes that atman is distinct from maya, with the ultimate goal being to realize the true nature of the self beyond the illusions of maya (cite: edge_020888). Ultimately, maya is seen as a power that creates the world and all its phenomena, with atman being the underlying reality that exists beyond maya (cite: edge_018157).
